Servings: 8
Active: 20m
Total: 1h

---

### Shopping List

**Lamb**

- 2 - Frenched Racks of Lamb
- 1 1/2 tsps - Kosher Salt
- 3/4 tsps - Black Pepper
- 1 tsp - Vegetable Oil

**Herb Coating**

- 3 - Large Garlic cloves
- 1/4 cup - Chopped Flat-Leaf Parsley
- 1 tbsp - Chopped Thyme
- 2 tsps - Chopped Rosemary
- 1/2 tsp - Koasher Salt
- 1/2 tsp - Black Pepper
- 1 1/2 tbsp - EVOO

---

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350&deg;F with rack in the middle. Finely chop garlic, parsley, thyme, and rosemary. Heat a 12-inch skillet with a drizzle of oil over high heat.

2. Season lamb with salt & pepper. Brown all sides in pan (about 10 minutes). Transfer browned lamb to a 13x9 roasting pan.

3. In a medium bowl, combine garlic, herbs, salt & pepper, and EVOO. Coat lamb with mixture, pressing to help adhere to meat. Roast 15m.

4. Cover lamb loosely with foil and roast until internal temperature reaches 120&deg;F for Medium Rare (about 5 - 10 more minutes). Let stand while covered for an additional 10 minutes for the internal temperature to rise.
